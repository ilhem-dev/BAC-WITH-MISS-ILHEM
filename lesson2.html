Complete sentence B using "providing that" or "as long as" appropriately:

Item 1

A: If the company's manager embezzles, the employees will leave.

B: providing that

Item 2

A: If students study regularly, they will pass the exam.

B: as long as

Item 3

A: Pupils will not cheat if the teacher promotes ethical thinking.

B: Providing that

Item 4

A: Providing that governments encourage whistle-blowing, corruption will decrease.

B: Corruption will decrease

Item 5

A: Providing that the company adheres to its stated code of ethics, it can establish a strong reputation for integrity within the industry.

B: as long as

Item 6

A: As long as executives prioritize corporate social responsibility, they will foster a culture of ethical awareness throughout the organization.

B: if

üîó Exercise 2: Combining Sentences

Combine the following sentences using the words in brackets (make changes if necessary):

Item 1

Combine: The teacher explains the lesson. The students understand. (Providing that)

Item 2

Combine: Ahmed exercises. Ahmed keeps a good shape. (As long as)

Item 3

Combine: You solve exercises. You understand the lesson. (Provided that)

Item 4

Combine: Police do their job. Thieves are in jail. (As long as)

Item 5

Combine: Companies encourage whistle-blowing. Companies flourish. (if)

Item 6

Combine: Ethical principles are maintained. Educators transmit the tradition of ethical thinking to each generation of students. (providing that)

Item 7

Combine: Ethical considerations are taken into account in every decision making process. The company demonstrates its commitment to responsible business practices. (providing that)

Item 8

Combine: A company can foster a culture of integrity and discourage bribery and favoritism. Ethical conduct is rewarded and unethical behavior is penalized. (Provided that)

Item 9

Combine: Financial institutions maintain their reputation and regulatory compliance. Money laundering risks are assessed and mitigated. (As long as)

Item 10

Combine: Any attempts at embezzlement are quickly detected and addressed. Strict auditing procedures are followed. (Providing that)

Paragraph 1

Unethical behaviors in the workplace (have) serious consequences. These actions (harm) a company's reputation and even (lead) to legal issues. Providing that organizations (prioritize) ethical conduct, they (foster) a culture of integrity and prevent such behaviors.

Paragraph 2

In the past, corruption in Nigeria deeply (impact) the nation. The country's resources (be misappropriated), leading to infrastructure decay and widespread inequality. As long as corruption (persist), it (hinder) economic growth, (erode) public trust, and (exacerbate) poverty.

Paragraph 3

Whistleblowing (be) crucial. If individuals (have) the means and protection to report misconduct, it (promote) transparency, accountability, and ethical standards. By encouraging reporting, we can (uncover) wrongdoing, (hold) wrongdoers accountable, and (maintain) trust and integrity within institutions. Whistleblowers (play) a significant role in upholding ethical values.
